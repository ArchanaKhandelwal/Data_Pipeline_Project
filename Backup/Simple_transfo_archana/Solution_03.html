Question:I have some extra time. Can I make my own meatballs for this recipe?

Question:Is harissa paste spicy?

Question:I can’t find harissa paste. What else can I use?

Answer:Chili oil or even sriracha could work here instead! Just adjust the amounts as necessary so that you don’t make it too spicy.

Question:How do you make this gluten-free and/or dairy-free?
